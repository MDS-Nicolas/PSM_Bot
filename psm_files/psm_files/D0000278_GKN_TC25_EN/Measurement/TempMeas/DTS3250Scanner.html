Temperature Measurement System

Scanivalve DTS3250/DTS4050

Only v3.4.3 or higher of the proDAS software has the ability to configure and acquire data from DTS3250 temperature scanners installed with versions 2.x and 3.x of the scanner firmware.

The Temperature Measurement System includes Scanivalve DTS3250/DTS4050 16/32/48 channel Digital Temperature Scanners (client-supplied).

The temperature scanner measures 16/32/48 temperature channels for proDAS. The scanner contains 16/32/48 Uniform Temperature References (UTRs) and a microprocessor. Each UTR has a thermocouple reference in contact with both thermocouple and copper junctions. The microprocessor corrects for sensor zero, span, and non linearity errors.

Channels are scanned by proDAS at up to 40 Hz. The scanners are mounted in the Test Cell and are powered by a 24 VDC power supply located in Equipment Rack CM4 in the Computer Room.

The scanner gathers data from thermocouples mounted on the engine or on the engine adapters.

The temperature scanner is connected to the proDAS Linux Host Computer through the Scanner Control Panel in equipment rack CM4, slot A1 and the Real-Time Ethernet Switch in Equipment Rack CM1 in the Computer Room. Measured temperatures are sent to proDAS using TCP/IP.
