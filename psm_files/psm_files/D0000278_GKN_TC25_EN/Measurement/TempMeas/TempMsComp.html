Temperature Measurement System

Components

The Temperature Measurement System contains the following major components:

Component Description
DTS3250/DTS4050 Digital Temperature Scanner (client-supplied) The temperature scanner measures 16 temperature channels for proDAS. The scanner gathers data from thermocouples mounted on the engine or on the engine adapters.
EX1048A Temperature Scanner The EX1048A temperature scanner is a 48 channel thermocouple scanner to measure temperatures from thermocouples. Each EX1048 measures 48 temperature channels for proDAS.

For more information on the Temperature Measurement System components, see Vendor Documentation.
