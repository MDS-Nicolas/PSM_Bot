Temperature Measurement System

EX1048A Temperature Scanners

There are four VTI Instruments 48 Channel Precision Thermocouple Instruments installed in the client SAS Rack Assembly. Each EX1048 measures 48 temperature channels for proDAS. The channels are scanned by proDAS at up to 200 Hz.

The EX1048 gathers data from type k thermocouples. The EX1048 is connected to the proDAS Real-Time Ethernet network via an Ethernet Switch in Equipment Rack CM1, in the Computer Room.

Calibration

The EX1048A temperature scanners must be calibrated once a year at the manufacturer, or at a recognised calibration house. The calibration information is stored in the temperature scanner.

To guarantee the published accuracies, on-site self-calibrations must be performed:

  • at least once a month
  • whenever there is a large change in temperature

For details on performing an on-site self-calibration, see the Ex1048A User Manual. The scanner channels may be audited by connecting a thermocouple simulator to the scanner, and comparing its value with the value displayed on proDAS. For details, see How To – Inspect the Calibration of a Temperature Scanner.
