Maintenance Schedule

The system maintenance tables below contain the maintenance schedule for each system. The schedule indicates the frequency requirements for the maintenance, the system/component requiring the maintenance, a description of the type of maintenance, and provide links to documentation required for conducting the maintenance.

The following systems contain MDS recommended maintenance:

Computer Configuration:

Host Computer

Topic Maintenance
As Required Annual
Checking the Disk Space on the Host Computer
 
Dusting and Visual Inspection of Internal Components
(Includes checking the cooling fans.)		  

Windows Computers

Topic Maintenance
Annual
Scan the Hard Drive for Errors
Dust and Inspect the Internal Components on a Computer
(Includes checking the cooling fans.)

Control/Monitoring Systems:

Throttle

Topic Maintenance
As Required Annual
Check the Throttle Quadrant Wiring
 
Set Up the Lever Potentiometer
 
Adjust the Actuator Override Stop Position
 
Inspect the Brushes on the Throttle Lever  
Clean the Throttle Quadrant  
Calibrate the Throttle Lever  
Check the Wiring in the Throttle Controller Enclosure Assembly  
Calibrate the Actuator  
Verify the Actuator Override System Operation  
Lubricate the Air Motor  
Check the Actuator Spur and Anti-Backlash Gears  
Check the Actuator Enclosure Assembly (Screws,Wires, Fasteners)  

Dynamic Data System:

Dynamic Data System (DDS)

Topic Maintenance
As Required
Dust and Verify the Internal Components on a Computer (Includes dusting and checking the cooling fans.)

Facility:

UPS

Topic Maintenance
Annual
Functional verification of the rectifier, inverter, and batteries by a UPS OEM representative

Measurement Systems:

Pneumatic Pressure Measurement

Topic Maintenance
Before Each Engine Test Annual
Inspect the Pneumatic Pressure Measurement System
Purge and Zero the Pressure Scanners
Check the Calibration of the Pressure Scanners  

Temperature Measurement

Topic Maintenance
As Required Before Each Test 1 Month 6 Months Annual
Inspect the Calibration of a Temperature Scanner
     
Inspect the Temperature Measurement System  
 
 
Perform a Self-Calibration of the Temperature Scanners
   
Troubleshoot Trigger Problems with the Temperature Scanners
       

proDAS Hardware Configuration:

Racks

Topic Maintenance
As Required
Inspect the Equipment Racks

Includes:
  • Inspect and clean the Cooling Fans
  • Inspect area for FOD
  • Dust the racks

VXI Hardware

Topic Maintenance
As Required 6 Months
Check the Operation of the VXI DIO Card  
Inspect and Clean the VXI chassis
 
Replace a VXI Card in the VXI chassis
