How To – Inspect and Clean the VXI Chassis

This How To provides the steps required to inspect and clean the VXI Chassis.

To Inspect and Clean the VXI Chassis

To prevent serious injury and/or irreparable damage to equipment, observe all manufacturer's warning and safety precautions during operation and/or maintenance.

Electrostatic discharge can damage components and/or boards on your computer system. To avoid damage from electrostatic discharge when handling components and/or boards/cards/modules, wear an electrostatic strap that is connected to a proper ground before removing the card from the VXI chassis. Store modules in an antistatic bag when the modules are not in use.

  1. Clean the mainframe chassis using an ESD safe vacuum cleaner to remove dust and dirt that may have been drawn in by the cooling fans.
  2. Check the filter and clean or replace as needed.
