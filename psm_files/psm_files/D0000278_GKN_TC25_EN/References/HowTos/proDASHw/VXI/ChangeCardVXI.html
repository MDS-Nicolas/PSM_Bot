How To – Replace a VXI Card in the VXI Chassis

This How To provides the steps required to replace a VXI card in the VXI chassis.

The modular design of the VXI system ensures quick access for changing and servicing of the cards in the VXI chassis.

To Replace a VXI Card

To prevent serious injury and/or irreparable damage to equipment, observe all manufacturer's warning and safety precautions during operation and/or maintenance.

Electrostatic discharge can damage components and/or boards on your computer system. To avoid damage from electrostatic discharge when handling components and/or boards/cards/modules, wear an electrostatic strap that is connected to a proper ground before removing the card from the VXI chassis. Store modules in an antistatic bag when the modules are not in use.

  1. Shut down proDAS. Use the How To – Power Down the Instrumentation System procedure.
  2. Remove the cables from the front of the card.

    3. Ensure the cables are propely labelled so they can be properly reconnected in the new card.

  3. Remove the defective card.
  4. Replace the card with an identical unit.

    Ensure that the correct address is set on the replacement module. Refer to the Chassis Assembly, VXI engineering drawing for card settings.

  5. Reconnect the cables to the front of the new card.

    6. If the wrong cables are installed on the new card, it will cause incorrect measurements and may result in damage to the card or the test article.

  6. Turn on proDAS. Use the How To – Power Up the Instrumentation System procedure.
  7. Configure proDAS. Use the How to Configure the VXI Chassis Using the Subsystem Editor procedure.
  8. Verify that the module is operating as expected.
