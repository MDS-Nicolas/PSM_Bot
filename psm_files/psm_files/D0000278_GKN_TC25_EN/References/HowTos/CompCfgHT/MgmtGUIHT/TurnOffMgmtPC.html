How To – Power Down the Management GUI Computer from the Windows Taskbar

This How To provides the steps required to power down off the Management GUI computer from the Windows Taskbar.

Before powering down the computer, a proper shutdown using the Windows taskbar is required.

proDAS must be reconfigured if the Management GUI Computer is powered down, rebooted or restarted.

To Power Down the Management GUI Computer from the Windows Taskbar

To power down the Management GUI Computer, follow the procedure that best suits the situation as described below:

  • the proDAS software is configured and scanning or NOT scanning
  • the proDAS software is NOT configured and NOT scanning

The proDAS Software is Configured and Scanning or NOT Scanning

  1. From the proDAS Management GUI application, click Exit ProDAS.
  2. On the Windows taskbar, click Start, and then click Shut Down.
    The computer shuts down the Operating System and turns itself off.
  3. Turn off the Management GUI Computer monitor by pushing the power button.

The proDAS Software is NOT Configured and NOT Scanning

  1. On the Windows taskbar, click Start, and then click Shut Down.
    The computer shuts down the Operating System and turns itself off.
  2. Turn off the Management GUI Computer monitor by pushing the power button.
