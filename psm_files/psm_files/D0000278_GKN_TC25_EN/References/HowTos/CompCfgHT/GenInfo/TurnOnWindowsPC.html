How To – Power Up the Computer

This How To provides the steps required to power up a computer that is running a Windows operating system.

To Power Up the Computer

Make sure that the computer and monitor are connected to a power source and that the associated keyboard, mouse and monitor cables are properly connected.

  1. Turn on the monitor by pressing the monitor's power button.
  2. Turn on the computer by pressing the power button on the front of the computer.
    After the computer has booted, the user is automatically logged in as engineer.
