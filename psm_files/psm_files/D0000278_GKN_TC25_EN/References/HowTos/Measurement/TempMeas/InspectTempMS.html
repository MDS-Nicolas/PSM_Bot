How To – Inspect the Temperature Measurement System

This How To provides the steps required to inspect the Temperature Measurement System.

To Inspect the Temperature Measurement System

To prevent injury and damage to equipment, the operation and or maintenance should be carried out by a qualified technician. Observe the manufacturer's maintenance and repair guidelines and precautions.

  1. Ensure all bolted connectors, wires and cables are fastened securely. Tighten any loose connectors and fasteners.
  2. Inspect all wires and cables for nicks, cuts, corrosion or any other sign of damage, and repair or replace as necessary.
