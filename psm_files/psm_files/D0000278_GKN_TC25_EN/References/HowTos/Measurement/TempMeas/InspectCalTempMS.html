How To – Inspect the Calibration of a Temperature Scanner

This How To provides the steps required to inspect the calibration of a temperature scanner.

To Inspect the Calibration of a Temperature Scanner

To prevent injury and damage to equipment, the operation and or maintenance should be carried out by a qualified technician. Observe the manufacturer's maintenance and repair guidelines and precautions.

  1. Connect a thermocouple simulator directly to the input connections on a temperature scanner.
  2. Ensure proDAS is scanning.
  3. Using the thermocouple simulator, inject several different temperatures over the measurement range of the channel and record the measurements displayed on proDAS.
  4. If the measurements do not not meet the accuracy specification, perform a self calibration and re-audit the channels. For details on performing a self-calibration, see the Ex1000A/EX1000A-TC User Manual.
  5. Return the scanner to the supplier if it still does not meet the accuracy specification.
