Engineering Drawings

A list of applicable engineering drawings is provided for each subsystem. The sheet numbers for each drawing are hyperlinked to the corresponding drawing.

The drawings can be viewed from this master list (arranged by subsystem) or from within the subsystem write-ups. To see a specific drawing, click on the drawing sheet number. The drawing will be displayed in the main viewing area.

The drawing nomenclature is listed as follows:

  • GA - General Assembly
  • SS - System Schematic
  • CA - Cable Assembly
  • WD - Wiring Diagram
