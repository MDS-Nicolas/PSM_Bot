Equipment Racks

Maintenance

To avoid electric shock, disconnect power from the racks until the dusting and inspection are completed.

Electrostatic discharge can damage components and/or boards. To avoid damage from electrostatic discharge when handling components and/or boards, wear an electrostatic strap that is connected to a proper ground.
