IRIG-B

Components

The IRIG-B System contains the following major components:

Component Description
GPS IRIG-B Receiver Card The GPS IRIG-B Receiver Card is installed in the Host Computer in equipment rack CM1 in the Computer Room. The card provides precise time and frequency to the Host Computer and peripheral data acquisition systems.
Low Frequency Distribution Amplifier (IRIGA/B or G) The IRIG-B amplifier is used to distribute the IRIG signal to any equipment requiring an IRIG-B signal. The amplifier is installed in Equipment Rack CM1.

For more information on the IRIG-B components, see Vendor Documentation.
