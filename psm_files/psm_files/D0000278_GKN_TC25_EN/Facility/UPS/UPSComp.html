Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS)

Components

The Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) System contains the following major components:

Component Description
10 KVA UPS The 10 KVA UPS is a UPS-Teknik KR 31 (3p-1p) 10-20 kVA series.
External Battery Cabinet The external battery cabinet provides up to 30 minutes (at half load) of back-up power to the 10 KVA UPS during a power outage. It is a custom made cabinet, which houses 40 12V batteries.
Rack Mounted External Battery Module The rack mountable external battery module provides up to 30 minutes (at half load) of back-up power to the 10 KVA UPS during a power outage. The module houses 8 12V batteries.
External Bypass 10 kVA 3 Phase/1 Phase The external bypass is used to connect to the main facility electrical power.
