Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS)

10 KVA UPS

The 10 KVA UPS is a UPS-Teknik KR31 (3p-1p) Series 10-20 KVA UPS. The UPS unit provides a 10 KVA sustained load for a minimum of 30 minutes to ensure that an engine can be safely shut down if a facility electrical power failure occurs. The UPS is installed in the Instrumentation Room.

The UPS provides back-up power for the following MDS supplied systems:

  • proDAS
  • Dynamic Data System (DDS)
  • Throttle system

The UPS supplies power to the client-supplied Distribution Panel, which distributes power to the Computer Room and the Control Room.

For operation details, see the UPS-Teknik User Manual.
