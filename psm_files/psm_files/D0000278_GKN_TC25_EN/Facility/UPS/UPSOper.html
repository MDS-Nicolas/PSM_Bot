Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS)

Operation

Facility emergency battery back-up power is supplied by the following UPS units:

  • 10 KVA UPS

The UPS units provide power for the following systems:

  • proDAS
  • Dynamic Data System (DDS)
  • Throttle System

During an active alarm, an alarm indicator is illuminated and an audible alarm sounds.

The UPS is monitored by the client-supplied FCS and the UPS status is displayed on the Power System screen.
