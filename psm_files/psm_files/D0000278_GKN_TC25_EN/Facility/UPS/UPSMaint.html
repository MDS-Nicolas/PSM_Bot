Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS)

Maintenance

The UPS contains lethal high voltage. Removal by unqualified personnel of the doors of the UPS can cause damage both to the operator and the equipment.

To prevent injury and damage to equipment, the operation and or maintenance should be carried out by a qualified technician.
